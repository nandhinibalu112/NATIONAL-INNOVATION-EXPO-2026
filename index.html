Our Vision

Transforming Student Innovation Into A National Movement

TECH AURA 2026 aims to bridge academia, startups, industries, incubators and innovators through a national innovation ecosystem.

Competition Event Flow

01

Registration

Teams register online detailing conceptual proposals across designated project tracks.

02

State Level

Initial screening and state-level virtual evaluations across all Indian states.

03

Zonal Competition

Zonal finals featuring physical presentations across East, West, North, and South zones.

04

National Finals

The grand faceoff of the remaining top teams at Chennai.

05

Awards

Distribution of prizes, strategic grants, and prototype scale incubators.

06

Networking

Direct interface with recruitment heads, VCs, startup mentors, and institutional partners.
