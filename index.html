About the Expo

A National Platform For Future Pioneers

This initiative transforms a traditional college project expo into a large-scale, innovation-focused ecosystem connecting students, startups, industry experts, and technology communities on one platform. The goal is to build a nationally recognized student innovation movement.

  • Create high-impact, national-level visibility for student innovation
  • Bring 4 competitive teams from every state across India
  • Connect forward-thinking students directly with active startup founders and MNC experts
  • Build industry-focused project exposure and robust real-world networking opportunities
  • Develop one of India's most vibrant and visible student innovation ecosystems

How It Works

Event Structure

01

State-Level Registration

Teams register and compete at the state level, representing colleges across all 28 participating states.

02

Zonal Competition

State winners advance to Zonal rounds — South, North, East & West — where the best innovators battle for the national stage.

03

National Showcase

Finalists take the main stage at Chennai — presenting to industry leaders, investors, and a 15,000+ student audience for national recognition.
